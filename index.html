Project 02

A Human-Centered Wellness Experience Platform

Role
UX/Interaction Design
Process/Impact

Led the end-to-end design of a wellness platform that empowers patients to monitor their well-being, manage symptoms, and track health trends over time. Mapped end-to-end user flows based on qualitative interviews and journey mapping, then developed and tested a functional prototype through multiple usability studies with stakeholders. Leveraged a human-centered design process combined with data-informed iteration loops, emotional usability and technical functionality validations to refine content hierarchy, interaction patterns, and visual accessibility. The platform achieved increased user engagement and retention upon initial testing, with feedback highlighting clarity and control, ease of use, and emotional reassurance through personalized symptom tracking.

Project 03

UX Design and Optimization for Internal Tooling

Role
UX Strategy, Information Architecture 
Process

Led the design of an internal software interface to streamline developer workflows by structuring message handling into clear, minimal sections. Prioritized intuitive navigation and simplicity though applied information architecture principles and card sorting to reduce cognitive load and accelerate task completion. Collaborated closely with engineering teams to align interface design with technical requirements, iteratively refining layouts to optimize clarity and usability within the minimalist constraints. The result is a clean, intuitive interface that improved developer efficiency, reduced errors in message processing, ensured seamless integration with backend systems and enhanced cross-team collaboration.
